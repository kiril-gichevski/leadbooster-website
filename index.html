Frequently asked questions

Who is LeadBooster for?

Anyone who manages enrichment APIs and runs outbound — cold email agencies, lead gen freelancers, growth hackers, and solo founders doing their own outbound. If you already have API accounts (Hunter, Apollo, OpenAI) and want a simpler, cheaper way to run them on your lead lists, LeadBooster is built for you.

Are there really no credits?

None. Ever. Some tools charge per row enriched on top of their monthly fee — the more you use it, the more you pay. LeadBooster is $29/mo per seat regardless of how many rows you run.

What does "Bring Your Own API Keys" mean?

You connect your existing Hunter, Apollo, and OpenAI accounts directly. You pay those providers at their actual published rates. We never mark up their prices, never see your keys, and never bundle costs into credits.

What's included in the $29/mo?

Unlimited rows, free web scraping, AI research agent, workflow builder with conditional logic, and 20+ integrations. The only extras are the API providers you choose — and you pay them directly at cost.

How is this different from Clay?

Clay bundles data and charges credits per row — you pay their markup on top of a $149–349/mo platform fee. LeadBooster connects your existing API accounts and charges a flat $29/mo. Same enrichment sources, no middleman pricing, no credit system.

How is this different from n8n or Make?

n8n and Make are general automation platforms — you can build anything, but lead enrichment requires code and complex multi-node workflows. LeadBooster is built only for lead enrichment, so setup takes minutes, not days. No code. No 47-node workflows. Just click the APIs you want to run.

What can I scrape for free?

Regular company websites — email extraction, phone numbers, tech stack detection, sitemap crawling. For Cloudflare-protected or JavaScript-heavy sites, connect your own Apify key. Free scraping covers the large majority of B2B use cases.

What's the money back guarantee?

If LeadBooster doesn't work for you within the first 30 days, email us and we'll refund you in full. No questions, no hoops, no friction.
