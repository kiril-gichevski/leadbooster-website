Frequently asked questions

Why is LeadBooster FREE?

We're in beta and building our user base. The platform will cost $25/month after beta ends (Q2 2026). Beta users get grandfathered pricing. Our business model is transparent: you pay us for the platform, you pay APIs directly for data‚Äîno markup, no hidden fees.

What does "Bring Your Own API Keys" mean?

Instead of buying "credits" from us with hidden API markups (like Clay), you buy API keys directly from providers like OpenAI, Hunter.io, or Apollo.io. You pay them at-cost, we never see your keys or charge markup. This keeps costs transparent and lets you own your provider relationships.

What features are completely FREE (no API costs)?

Multi-page web scraping, sitemap crawler, LinkedIn company scraper, and basic email verification are 100% FREE‚Äîno API keys needed. Research Agent and AI agents require your OpenAI/Anthropic keys (you pay them directly, typically $20-50/month).

How is this different from Clay.com?

Clay charges $349-800/month with a credit system that hides API costs. LeadBooster is FREE (or $25/month after beta) and you bring your own API keys‚Äîno markup, full transparency. Plus, we have built-in FREE multi-page scraping that Clay doesn't offer.

Do I need coding skills?

No! LeadBooster works entirely in Google Sheets with a simple sidebar interface. If you can use Google Sheets, you can use LeadBooster. Select your columns, configure tasks, and hit "Start"‚Äîthat's it.

What happens to my API keys?

Your API keys are stored securely in YOUR Google account using Google Apps Script's PropertiesService. We never see them, never store them on our servers, and never use them for anything except your enrichment tasks. You can delete them anytime.

Can I process large datasets?

Yes! LeadBooster processes up to 50,000 rows per day per user with background execution and auto-resume. You can close your browser and come back later‚Äîyour data will be enriched.

What if I run out of API credits with a provider?

Since you own the API keys, you control your spending. If you run out of Hunter.io credits, just upgrade your Hunter plan directly‚Äîno need to involve us. You stay in full control of your provider relationships and budgets.

What's the "Research Agent"?

It's our Claygent alternative. Give it a research prompt (e.g., "Find CEO email for {{Company Name}}"), and it Googles the answer, scrapes relevant websites, and synthesizes findings with AI. Platform is FREE‚Äîyou just need OpenAI + Serper.dev API keys (~$25/month).

When does the beta end and pricing start?

We plan to introduce the $25/month Pro plan in Q2 2026. Beta users who sign up now will get grandfathered pricing or extended FREE access as a thank-you for early support. You'll get at least 30 days notice before any charges.
