How to Connect HubSpot

Create a HubSpot Private App and add your access token in minutes

Overview

To push enriched leads from LeadBooster directly into HubSpot, you need to create a Private App in HubSpot and generate an access token. This token lets LeadBooster securely read and write contacts, companies, deals, and leads to your CRM.

The whole process takes about 5 minutes. Follow the steps below — each one includes a screenshot.

1

Go to Development → Legacy Apps

In your HubSpot account, click the Settings icon in the top navigation bar. In the left sidebar, find Integrations and click on Legacy Apps.

2

Click "Create a Legacy App"

On the Legacy Apps page, click the "Create a legacy app" button to start setting up a new integration.

3

Choose "Private App"

When prompted to select the app type, choose Private. This creates a direct token-based integration — no OAuth flow required.

4

Add a Name

Give your app a recognizable name like "LeadBooster Integration". This is only visible inside your HubSpot account. You can optionally add a logo and description.

5

Add Required Scopes

Switch to the Scopes tab. Search for and enable each of the following permissions. LeadBooster needs these to read and write CRM data on your behalf.

crm.objects.contacts.read crm.objects.contacts.write crm.objects.companies.read crm.objects.companies.write crm.objects.deals.read crm.objects.deals.write crm.objects.owners.read crm.objects.leads.read crm.objects.leads.write crm.objects.orders.read crm.objects.orders.write
6

Create the App

Click the "Create app" button in the top right. A confirmation dialog will appear listing all the scopes you selected — review them and confirm to proceed.

7

Copy Your Access Token

Once the app is created, HubSpot will display your access token. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard. Treat this token like a password — do not share it publicly.

8

Paste Your Token in LeadBooster

Open LeadBooster in Google Sheets. Go to Settings → Integrations → HubSpot and paste your access token into the field. Click Save — your connection is now live.

What You Can Do Now

With HubSpot connected, LeadBooster can:

  • Push enriched contacts directly into HubSpot as Contacts and Companies
  • Create and update deals based on your workflow enrichment results
  • Sync lead data without leaving Google Sheets
  • Run conditional workflows that only push qualified leads — saving API credits

Troubleshooting

If LeadBooster shows a connection error after adding the token:

  • Make sure all required scopes are enabled — even one missing scope can cause failures
  • Check that you copied the full token with no extra spaces
  • Verify your HubSpot account has admin permissions to create Private Apps
  • If the token stops working, you can regenerate it from the same Private App page in HubSpot
  • Contact us at contact@leadbooster.dev if you're still stuck
