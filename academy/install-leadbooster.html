How to Install LeadBooster

2-minute setup — directly inside Google Sheets, no new platform to learn

Overview

LeadBooster installs as a Google Sheets add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace. Once installed, it opens as a sidebar inside any Google Sheet you already use. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.

1

Go to the Marketplace and Click Install

Open the LeadBooster listing on the Google Workspace Marketplace and click the blue Install button.

2

Click Continue in the Popup

A popup window will appear asking you to confirm the installation. Click Continue to proceed.

3

Accept the Permissions

Google will show a list of permissions LeadBooster needs. Click Allow to grant them — LeadBooster needs these to read your lead data and write enrichment results back into your sheet.

4

Open the Sidebar in Google Sheets

Open any Google Sheet, click Extensions in the top menu → LeadBooster → Open Sidebar. The LeadBooster panel will appear on the right — you're ready to go.

What's Next?

Now that LeadBooster is installed:

  • Add your API keys — connect Hunter, Apollo, OpenAI and any providers you already use
  • Build your first workflow — set up your enrichment sequence with conditional logic
  • Connect HubSpot — push enriched leads directly into your CRM

Troubleshooting

If LeadBooster doesn't appear in the Extensions menu:

  • Refresh the page and wait a few seconds — the add-on sometimes takes a moment to appear
  • Make sure you're signed in with the same Google account you used to install
  • Check that your Google Workspace admin hasn't restricted add-on installs
  • Contact us at contact@leadbooster.dev if you're still stuck
